ABOUT US
About the
Company

Our company was founded with the aim to raise awareness of diabetes and problems that people suffering from diabetes type 1 (T1D) face with on a daily basis within our local community through connecting and exchanging experience with organizations in Serbia, the region, and international organizations.
‍
Our intention was to deal with the problems that are part of everyday life of people suffering from diabetes. Even some of us have witnessed that daily struggle because people close to us suffering from this condition have found themselves in situations when they were helpless and needed adequate help.

They were surrounded by strangers who were not informed (and sympathetic) enough to realize the problem and act adequately. Our communication and business skills help us work successfully as a team. We consider ourselves to be sociable, approachable, communicative, co-operative, organized, professional and creative. Since the beginning we have managed to keep outstanding work atmosphere, to be innovative and socially responsible.

OUR PRODUCTS
Explore our
Products

Our product, candy bracelet that contains accacia honey, agar-agar and raspberry, is a life-saving first aid for type 1 diabetics when they find themselves in a hypoglycemic state. Bracelet is designed to allow a type 1 diabetic to react quickly in urgency moments to avoid falling into hypoglycemic coma, and it is also emergency call when diabetic needs help in a public place where he is surrounded by unknown people who are thanked to design able to recognize a problem.
